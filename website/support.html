Support

Help with your account, garage, meets, and more.

Find answers to common questions, see what details to include when you contact us, and get back into Empire Connect faster.

Best ways to get help

Account support

If you cannot sign in, reset your password, or access your profile, contact support and include the email tied to your account.

Garage and community issues

If a build, post, image, or profile update is missing, send a screenshot, your device model, and a short description of what went wrong.

Meet check-ins and event questions

If a QR check-in fails or an event looks wrong, include the meet title, date, and what happened when you tried to check in.

Merch and VIP questions

For merch, VIP, or purchase issues, include your order or membership details along with any helpful screenshots or screen recordings.

Support checklist

  • App version or beta build number
  • iPhone model and iOS version
  • Short description of the issue
  • What you expected to happen
  • Screenshots or screen recording if available

FAQ

Answers to common questions.

How do I reset my password?

Use the “Forgot password?” option from the login screen. Follow the email link and return to the app to complete recovery.

Why is my vehicle not showing up?

Try refreshing the app, confirm you are on the correct account, and report the issue if a newly added build still does not sync.

Why didn’t a meet QR check-in work?

Make sure the QR code matches the correct event and that your camera permissions are enabled for Empire Connect.

Where do I report a beta bug?

Contact support with your device details, steps to reproduce, and screenshots or screen recordings if you have them.

Permissions you may be asked about

Camera

Empire Connect may request camera access so members can scan meet QR codes for event check-in.

Calendar

The app may request calendar access so users can save club meets and reminders directly to their device calendar.

Notifications

Push notifications help surface community activity, event reminders, and updates tied to the user’s account activity.

Operational status

All systems operational

If something looks broken or your data seems off, send us a quick description, timestamps, and screenshots so we can investigate.
